# GREA Portal — Phase 1 Prototype Review: How Feedback Works

*Please read before exploring the prototype. ~2 minutes.*

---

## What you're looking at

This is the **Phase 1 interactive prototype** of the GREA Portal — a national,
read-only "mirror" that lets you look up contacts, deals, and shared lists
across every GREA office in one place.

- **It runs on dummy data.** Names, deals, and numbers are placeholders for
  demonstration only. Do not evaluate the *data* — evaluate the *structure*.
- **It is a mirror, not a CRM.** It displays information; your office system
  remains the source of truth. You won't enter or edit live records here.

## What we need from you right now

Phase 1 has **one job: alignment.** We need to confirm the portal shows the
**right fields and views** for cross-office lookup *before* real data goes in.

> The single question to keep in mind:
> **"Does this show the information I'd actually need to find a contact or deal
> in another office?"**

Please **don't** send feature wish-lists, design opinions, or workflow ideas
yet — there's a place for those (see *Parking Lot* below), but they're not the
focus of this round.

## How to give feedback — one channel only

**Use the in-app feedback tools. Do not send feedback by email, text, or call.**
Keeping it in one place is the only way every comment gets tracked and nothing
gets lost.

1. **Found a problem with a specific contact or deal?** Click the
   **"Report an issue"** link on that item.
2. **Anything else** (a missing field, a confusing view, a general
   observation)? Click the **Feedback** button in the app, add a short title
   and description.

The more specific, the better: tell us *what page*, *what you expected*, and
*what you saw*.

## Who talks to whom (the funnel)

To keep this manageable across all offices, communication flows in one
direction:

```
Brokers  ──►  Office Admin  ──►  Executive Appointee  ──►  Ariel (build)
```

- **Brokers:** submit feedback in-app; route questions to your Office Admin.
- **Office Admins:** consolidate your office's feedback and resolve duplicates.
- **Executive Appointee:** makes the final call on any business decision
  (which fields, what "freshness" means, update frequency) and hands Ariel a
  single, agreed specification.
- **Ariel:** receives the agreed spec and builds. Ariel does not arbitrate
  debates between offices — that's resolved internally first.

This isn't bureaucracy; it's what keeps the project on schedule and on budget.

## In scope vs. the Parking Lot

| ✅ In scope for Phase 1 feedback | 🅿️ Parking Lot (noted, but later) |
|---|---|
| Missing or wrong **fields** on a contact/deal | New features and integrations |
| Views or columns that don't match how you search | Visual / branding preferences |
| Anything confusing or unclear in the layout | Workflow & automation requests |
| Data you'd expect to see but can't | Anything requiring real/live data |

Parking-lot ideas are welcome — submit them the same way and we'll log them for
the right phase.
